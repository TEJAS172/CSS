There are 4 different combination in css..

  1. Desendent Selector(USING SPACE)
  2. Child Selector(USING > SYMBOL)
  3. Adjecent Sibling Selector(USING + SYMBOL)
  4. General Sibling Selector(USING = SYMBOL)

Desendent Selector (USING SPACE) Example..

First H3 Tag.. (child element)

First Paragraph.. (child element)

Second H3 Tag.. (child element)

Second paragraph.. (child element)

Third H3 Tag..(child element)

Third paragraph..(child element)

Fourth H3 Tag..(Grandchild element)

Fourth paragraph(Grand child element)

Fifth h3 tag..(Child element)

Fifth paragraph..(child element)
